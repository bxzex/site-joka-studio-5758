Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to secure your preferred time and technician.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment to avoid a cancellation fee.

Do you offer nail art?

Yes! Our technicians are skilled in various forms of nail art. Please mention you'd like nail art when booking so we can allocate the appropriate amount of time.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is street parking available near the studio, as well as a paid parking garage within walking distance.
